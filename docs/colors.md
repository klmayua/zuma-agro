## Color Usage Rules

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text:** Charcoal (#18211D)
- **Secondary Text:** Slate (#6B7280)
- **Inverse Text:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Link Text:** Primary Green (#005A43)
- **Link Hover:** Dark Green (#004D3A)

### Background Colors
- **Page Background:** Ivory (#F8F8F4)
- **Card Background:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Input Background:** Stone (#EAEDE8)
- **Primary Background:** Zuma Deep Green (#006B53)
- **Secondary Background:** Heritage Gold (#C9A03D)

### Border Colors
- **Default Border:** Stone (#EAEDE8)
- **Focus Border:** Primary Green (#005A43)
- **Error Border:** Rich Red (#B63A3A)
- **Gold Border (Hover):** Heritage Gold (#C9A03D)

### State Colors
- **Success:** Primary Green (#005A43)
- **Warning:** Heritage Gold (#C9A03D)
- **Error:** Rich Red (#B63A3A)
- **Info:** Slate (#6B7280)
