# Design System Audit

## Style Guideline Overview
The project implements a **Premium Agrarian Heritage** style guide. It projects the stability of an institutional investment trust combined with the organic nature of large-scale agriculture.

### Colors
- **Primary (Deep Green):** `#005a43` (from the logo). Used for structural boundaries and brand identity.
- **Secondary (Heritage Gold):** `#a8862d` / `#ffd169`. Reserved for CTAs, highlighted metric numbers, and active indicator states.
- **Tertiary (Impact Red):** `#d32f2f` / `#ba1a1a`. Used for risks, downward trends, or critical alerts.
- **Background (Off-White Base):** `#f8faf6`. A warm ivory canvas reducing visual strain.
- **Card Surfaces (Ivory Muted):** `#eceeeb` / `#ffffff` with a 1px border in Stone (`#bfc9c2`).
- **Text (Charcoal/Black):** `#191c1a` for maximum readability.

### Typography
- **Headlines (Classical Serif):** `Libre Caslon Text`. Evokes trust, history, and established authority.
- **Body & Labels (Geometric Sans):** `Manrope` / `Inter`. Clean Grotesque typeface ensuring data-dense charts and tabular info remain crisp and readable.

### Spacing & Grid
- **Base Increment:** 8px
- **Container Maximum Width:** 1280px
- **Outer Margin (Desktop):** 64px
- **Outer Margin (Mobile):** 20px
- **Gutter:** 24px
- **Radiuses:** Soft corners (4px base, 8px for cards, 12px for larger containers).
