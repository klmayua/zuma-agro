# Platform Architecture

## Information Architecture
The platform is organized into three key sections: public marketing pages, an investor gateway, and a multi-step institutional enrollment flow.

### 1. Public Marketing Portal
- **Home**: `/` - Sovereign Landing Page containing high-level value propositions, agro-industrial multipliers, and strategic SBUs.
- **About**: `/about` - Context on history, vision, mandate, and leadership (Strategic Alignment Page).
- **Framework**: `/strategic-framework` - Detailed agrarian transformation strategy and deployment blueprints.
- **Platform**: `/platform-architecture` - Digital operating models and logistics hub architecture.
- **Governance**: `/governance-controls` - Boards, ESG standards, risk management, and compliance controls.
- **Impact**: `/impact` - Multiplier effects, job creation estimates, and regional development analytics.

### 2. Investor Portal
- **Investor Intelligence Room**: `/investor-intelligence` - Data room, DFI relations, capital allocations, and secure investment metrics.
- **Institutional Partnerships**: `/institutional-partnerships` - Frameworks for sovereign partnerships and commercial coordination.

### 3. Institutional Enrollment Flow (Multi-step Form)
- **Step 1: Identification**: `/enrollment-step-1` - Form for institutional registry and stakeholder categorization.
- **Step 2: Architecture**: `/enrollment-step-2` - Coordination parameters and SBU alignment forms.
- **Step 3: Alignment**: `/enrollment-step-3` - National expansion targets and asset commitment.
- **Step 4: Final Verification**: `/enrollment-step-4` - Submission preview, document checks, and verification dashboard.

### 4. Raw Documents & Assets
- **Transformation Framework Markdown**: `/framework-document` - Serves the raw markdown strategy.
- **ChatGPT Mock Image Screen**: `/chatgpt-image` - Embeds the ChatGPT-generated flow diagram screen.
- **Reference Image Screen**: `/image-reference` - Embeds the newly added graphic image screen.
